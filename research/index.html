My Research

My research interests include:

  • Highway Safety Analysis.
  • GIS Applications in Transportation.
  • Application of Statistics, Data Mining and Machine Learning in Transportation.
  • Econometric Methods for TransportationData Analysis.
  • Operations Research in Transportation Engineering.
  • Traffic Operations and Analysis, Simulation, Bicycle, and Pedestrian Studies.
  • Public /Transit Transportation.
  • Traffic Demand Forecasting and Modeling.
