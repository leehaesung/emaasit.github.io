Who is Daniel Emaasit?
A Data Geek in Transportation Engineering

Daniel Emaasit is currently a Ph.D student of Civil & Transportation Engineering at University of Nevada Las Vegas (UNLV). He is a graduate research assistant in the Transportation Research Center under the surpervision of Dr. Alexander Paz. His research interests include database design, data mining, advanced analytics, predicitive analytics, machine learning, statistical modeling and business intelligence.
