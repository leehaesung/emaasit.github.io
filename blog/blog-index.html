My Blog

I discuss my research on this blog about topics related to R, machine learning, data mining, data visualisation, Big Data and much more. I encourage you to give your feedback on the comments section at the end of each post.

Modeling Dynamic Panel Data in R

2015-05-01

In this post, we focus on two methods for estimating unobserved effects panel data models that are at least as common as first differencing. Although these methods are somewhat harder to describe and implement, several econometrics packages support them.

Principal Component Analysis in R

2015-04-25

In this post, I will show you how to do Principal Component Analysis (PCA) in R. PCA is concerned with explaining the variance-covariance structure of a set of variables through a few linear combinations of these variables.

Transformation to near Normality of Multivariate Data Using R

2015-04-01

In this post, I will show you how to use R to verify the assumptions of normality in multivariate data. In addition, we shall go through methods for transforming non-normal observations into observations that are approximately normal.
